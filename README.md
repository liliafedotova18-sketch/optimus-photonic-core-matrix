# Optimus Bio-Inspired Structural Systems

**Multifunctional Composite Elements for Next-Generation Humanoid Robotics**

Research concept exploring the integration of structural, sensing, and protective functions within a single advanced composite component.

---

## Concept Overview

This project investigates bio-inspired design principles for humanoid robotics. Inspired by natural muscle-tendon systems, the goal is to create lightweight structural elements that combine mechanical performance with embedded intelligence and environmental resilience.

Key areas of exploration:
- Advanced composite architectures for high-load applications
- Integrated sensing capabilities for real-time feedback
- Intelligent material solutions for thermal and electromagnetic management

---

## Project Goals

- Develop lightweight, high-performance structural components
- Explore ways to embed functional capabilities without compromising mechanical integrity
- Study multi-functional material systems for future robotic platforms

---
